Company history

Who we are!

  • March 1993

    Our Humble Beginnings

    Founded in the year 1993 by Spiro Denaj the company was signed with the name ALIFA Export GmbH, in Tirana, which main activites were trading of industrial equipment and transportation services.

  • May 2009

    Company transformation

    When reason of age forced the founder to leave and the junior took over in 2009, the company changed its activity in becoming one of the leading specialist in the field of itermediary operations, among others, for many albanian companies.
